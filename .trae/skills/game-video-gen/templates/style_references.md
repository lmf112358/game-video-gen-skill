## Camera Language for Marketing Videos

### 10-Second Video Structure

For a 10s marketing video, the recommended structure is:

```
0-2s:  Establishing shot (character in scene)
2-5s:  Build-up (channeling energy, preparing attack)
5-8s:  Climax (signature move execution)
8-10s: Resolution (aftermath, lingering effects)
```

### Camera Transitions

| Transition | Description | Duration |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| Push-in | Slow zoom toward character | 2-3s |
| Orbit | Camera circles character | 2-3s |
| Follow | Camera tracks with movement | 1-2s |
| Impact zoom | Quick zoom on hit moment | 0.5s |
| Pull-back | Reveal the full scene | 2-3s |

## Marketing Video Tone Guide

| Tone | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Epic | Grand scale, sweeping camera, dramatic lighting | New character reveals, major updates |
| Intense | Fast cuts, heavy effects, powerful impacts | Combat-focused, DPS characters |
| Mysterious | Dark atmosphere, slow reveals, subtle effects | Story characters, villain reveals |
| Warm | Soft lighting, gentle motion, inviting atmosphere | Support characters, casual content |
| Cool | Stylish angles, neon effects, urban vibe | Urban/sci-fi characters |
